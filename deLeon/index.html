About Me

Me

I’m an introverted sophomore pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology in Sta. Cruz, Laguna. I was born and raised in Cavinti, Laguna, and I’ve lived here my whole life; born on December 9, 2003, and still calling this quiet town home.
Fun fact: I’m actually a shifter—I spent one semester in BSTM (Tourism Management) before realizing my heart (and brain) belonged in tech. Turns out, Designing web feels more rewarding than memorizing countries and destinations!

My hobbies

Unlike most guys my age, my hobbies are pretty low-key and indoors. I love binge-watching K-dramas and movies, scrolling through social media (probably more than I should), and just enjoying quiet time. I’ll admit (*sigh) it’s not the healthiest routine, but I’m trying to balance it out with more productive habits.

My Programming Journey (So Far…)

Here’s the real talk: I struggle with understanding the deeper concepts of programming languages. Honestly, my skills are still below beginner level, and sometimes it feels like my brain just refuses to process loops or algorithms. But here’s the thing, I’m passionate about learning. Even if it takes me longer, I’m determined to put in the work, piece by piece, until I can finally become the web designer I want to be. Slow progress is still progress, right?
